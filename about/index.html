Hi, I'm Brian. Among my hobbies are podcasting with my wife Melissa, reading, and building websites.

To celebrate the 372 Pages We'll Never Get back podcast finishing their 10th book, I wrote what amounts to fan fiction. It seems fitting to present a story cribbed from other stories and geared for the reaction of, "I get that reference!"

To close another circle, the stripped-down role-playing game format I used is borrowed from the Flip the Table podcast, which was inspired in part by Mystery Science Theatre 3000. (The hosts riff on cheesy, weird, and obscure board games, then conclude with a game show. The podcast has ended, but I highly recommend listening through the archive.)

The benefits to this format are that I don't have to be creative, and I don't have to be correct. You can assume any mistakes are purposeful and ironic.
