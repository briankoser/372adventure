To celebrate the 372 Pages We'll Never Get back podcast finishing their 10th book, I present this 372 Pages fan-fiction role-playing game. It seemed fitting to write a story by uncreatively borrowing from other stories and stuffing it full of references.

To close another circle, the format of the game is adapted from the Flip the Table podcast, which was inspired in part by Mystery Science Theatre 3000. (The hosts riff on cheesy, weird, and obscure board games, then conclude with a game show.) The podcast has ended, but I highly recommend listening through the archive. (Start with Gone Birding! or The McDonald's Game.)
